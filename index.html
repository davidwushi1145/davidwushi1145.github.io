Biography

I am currently a Master student in the School of Information Science and Technology at ShanghaiTech University, supervised by Prof. Jie Zheng. I received my B.S. degree from Yunnan University in 2025.

My research interests include Knowledge Graph (Reasoning, Completion, Alignment) and AI for Science (Drug Discovery, Scientific Discovery). I am passionate about open source and have contributed significantly to various projects on GitHub.
